# Trending Visual Styles (2025-2026)

Current aesthetic trends for AI-generated images. Use these as inspiration when the user wants something fresh or doesn't have a specific style in mind.

---

## Ghibli-Inspired Portrait

Soft watercolor anime style with warm tones, pastoral backgrounds. Hugely popular for social profile transformations.

**Prompt pattern:**
```
A Studio Ghibli-inspired portrait of [subject] in a [pastoral/magical setting].
Soft watercolor textures, warm golden tones, gentle wind effects.
Detailed background with lush greenery and soft cloud formations.
Hand-painted anime aesthetic with visible brushwork. Warm, nostalgic mood.
```

**Best backend:** Gemini (more expressive atmospheric quality)

---

## Action Figure / Toy-ification

Turns people or characters into collectible action figures with packaging, accessories, and career props. Massive on LinkedIn and personal branding.

**Prompt pattern (GPT — better for text on packaging):**
```
Background: toy store shelf display.
Subject: a collectible action figure of [character description] inside clear plastic blister packaging on a branded cardboard backing.
Key details: the cardboard backing reads "[CHARACTER NAME]" in bold toy-brand font at the top. Accessories visible in the package: [3-4 career/personality-relevant items]. Figure is in a dynamic pose wearing [outfit].
Style: photorealistic product photography of an actual toy. Professional studio lighting.
Constraints: packaging text must be perfectly legible. No other figures visible.
```

---

## AI Polaroid / Vintage Instant Photo

Faded colors, soft lighting, retro framing. Taps into nostalgia.

**Prompt pattern:**
```
A vintage Polaroid photograph of [subject/scene]. Slightly faded colors with
a warm yellow tint. Soft, slightly overexposed lighting. The white Polaroid
border frames the image. Handwritten caption below reads "[caption text]".
Film grain visible. The photo sits on a [wooden table/cork board/scrapbook page].
```

**Best backend:** Either (GPT for readable caption text, Gemini for authentic photo feel)

---

## Cyberpunk / Neon Noir

Neon reflections on wet surfaces, dark urban backdrops, holographic elements.

**Prompt pattern:**
```
A cyberpunk cityscape at night. Neon signs reflect off rain-slicked streets in
shades of electric blue, hot pink, and violet. [Subject] stands beneath a
glowing holographic billboard. Volumetric fog catches the neon light.
Shot on 35mm film, slightly grainy. Blade Runner aesthetic with modern detail.
Dramatic rim lighting from the neon sources.
```

**Best backend:** Gemini (stronger atmospheric lighting)

---

## Editorial Illustration

Clean, modern editorial art for articles, presentations, and professional content.

**Prompt pattern:**
```
A modern editorial illustration for [publication/topic].
The scene depicts [visual metaphor for the concept].
Clean lines, a limited palette of [2-3 colors] with subtle paper texture.
Flat design with thoughtful use of negative space.
The composition leaves breathing room on the [left/right] third for text overlay.
Professional, sophisticated, suitable for a tech publication or business report.
```

**Best backend:** Either (GPT for consistency, Gemini for expressiveness)

---

## Split Composition / Before-After

Vertical split showing contrasting versions. Powerful for transformation stories.

**Prompt pattern (GPT):**
```
Background: clean, split vertically down the center.
Subject: the same [person/object/scene] shown in two states.
Left half: [before state — e.g., cluttered desk, stressed expression, old design].
Right half: [after state — e.g., organized workspace, confident smile, modern design].
Key details: a subtle dividing line or gradient transition between the halves.
Matching scale and angle on both sides for direct comparison.
Style: [photorealistic/illustrated]. Clean, professional.
```

**Best backend:** GPT (better spatial consistency between halves)

---

## Trading Card / RPG Layout

Character centered with borders, stats, icons, text areas. Gamified personal branding.

**Prompt pattern (GPT — text-heavy):**
```
Background: dark textured card surface with metallic gold border.
Subject: a fantasy trading card featuring [character description] as a [class/role].
The character portrait fills the upper 60% of the card.
Key details: card title "[NAME]" in metallic serif font at top.
Stats bar at bottom: [ATK: 85 | DEF: 72 | INT: 95 | CHA: 88].
Small icons for class abilities along the bottom edge.
Card rarity indicator: [gold star/diamond] in top-right corner.
Style: premium collectible card game art. Rich colors, detailed illustration.
Constraints: all text must be perfectly legible. Professional card game quality.
```

**Best backend:** GPT (text-heavy layout requires reliable text rendering)

---

## Minimalist Brand Assets

Clean silhouettes, balanced negative space, intentional typography. For logos, templates, and brand systems.

**Prompt pattern:**
```
Subject: a minimalist [logo/icon/brand mark] for [brand name/concept].
Key details: clean geometric shapes, strong silhouette that works at any size.
Balanced negative space. [2-3 color] palette maximum.
The word "[BRAND]" in [font style] below the mark.
Style: flat vector-like, no gradients, no 3D effects, no shadows.
Constraints: white background. Must be reproducible as a vector.
```

**Best backend:** GPT (consistency, text rendering)

---

## Anti-AI Aesthetic

Deliberately imperfect images that feel human — grain, blur, light leaks. Counters the "too perfect" AI look.

**Prompt pattern:**
```
A candid photograph of [subject/scene] that feels completely unplanned.
Visible film grain (ISO 800+), slight motion blur on the edges,
a warm light leak bleeding in from the left side.
Slightly underexposed shadows. Shot on a disposable camera or early 2000s
digital point-and-shoot. The framing is imperfect — subject slightly
off-center, horizon not quite level. Authentic, raw, unedited feel.
```

**Best backend:** Gemini (more natural imperfection, less "cleaned up")

---

## When to Suggest These

| User context | Suggest |
|-------------|---------|
| Personal branding, LinkedIn content | Action Figure, Trading Card, Split Composition |
| Tech blog, newsletter, presentations | Editorial Illustration, Minimalist Brand |
| Lifestyle brand, Gen Z audience | AI Polaroid, Anti-AI Aesthetic |
| Gaming, entertainment, fun content | Trading Card, Cyberpunk |
| Transformation story, coaching | Split Composition, Before-After |
| Brand identity, logo exploration | Minimalist Brand Assets |
| Social media engagement bait | Ghibli Portrait, Action Figure |
