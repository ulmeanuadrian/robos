# Starter Packs

Pre-configured brand context for common business types.

## Usage
1. Pick a starter pack that matches your business
2. Copy the files to your brand/ directory:
   ```
   cp skills/_catalog/starter-packs/consultant/* brand/
   ```
3. Open Claude Code and customize:
   ```
   "Review my brand context and adjust it based on what I tell you about my business"
   ```

## Available Packs
- **consultant/** - Solo consultants and coaches
- **agency/** - Small marketing/creative agencies (2-10 people)
- **ecommerce/** - Online store owners (Shopify, WooCommerce, etc.)
- **creator/** - Content creators, YouTubers, newsletter writers

## What's Included
Each pack contains three files:
- `voice.md` - How the brand sounds (tone, vocabulary, personality)
- `audience.md` - Who the brand talks to (demographics, pain points, habits)
- `positioning.md` - Where the brand sits in the market (differentiators, competitors)

## What's NOT Included
- `samples.md` - Writing samples are too specific to template. Add your own.
- `assets.md` - Visual assets depend on your brand. Fill in after setup.

## Customization
These packs are starting points, not final drafts. After copying, use the
brand skills to refine them:
- `brand-voice` to extract your real voice from existing content
- `brand-audience` to build a detailed ICP through interview
- `brand-positioning` to sharpen your market angle
