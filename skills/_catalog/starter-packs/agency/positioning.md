# Positioning

## One-Liner
Big agency thinking, startup speed. We're the marketing team mid-market
companies wish they could hire -- senior strategists and creators who
move fast and own the outcome.

## Value Proposition
You get a full senior team without the overhead of building one. No
juniors learning on your account. No slow approval chains. Every person
on your project has 5+ years in their discipline and the authority to
make decisions.

What clients get:
- Strategy that connects to revenue, not just impressions
- Creative that actually looks like their brand, not a template
- Reporting they can take to their board without editing
- A team that brings ideas every month, not just status updates
- Flexibility to scale up for launches and scale down when quiet

## Differentiators
1. **All-senior team.** No account coordinators, no interns on your account.
   The people in the pitch are the people doing the work. You'll know
   them by name.
2. **Speed.** We make decisions in hours, not weeks. No 6-layer approval
   process. Brief in the morning, first draft by end of day.
3. **Outcome-accountable.** We tie our work to metrics that matter --
   leads, revenue, pipeline -- not vanity numbers. We'll show you
   what's working and kill what isn't.
4. **Industry context.** We go deep in 3-5 verticals instead of being
   generalists. When we work with you, we already understand your buyer.
5. **Transparent pricing.** Flat monthly retainer. No surprise line items.
   No charging for a "revision round" when the brief changed.

## Competitor Landscape
- **Large agencies** (WPP, Omnicom, Publicis networks): enterprise process,
  enterprise prices, enterprise speed (slow). You're positioned as: same
  caliber talent, 3x faster, half the price, and your account actually
  matters to us.
- **Freelancers and solopreneurs**: great for specific tasks but can't
  provide integrated strategy + execution. Drop off when they get busy.
  You're positioned as: reliability and breadth of a team, without the
  agency overhead.
- **In-house teams**: limited perspectives, struggle to hire specialists.
  You're positioned as: the depth and variety they can't build internally,
  plus an outside perspective that prevents echo chambers.
- **Automation tools** (HubSpot, Mailchimp, Hootsuite): tools without
  strategy. You're positioned as: we use the tools too, but we also
  know what to put in them and why.

## Category
Boutique performance agency. Small enough to care, experienced enough to
deliver. We're building the category between "expensive big agency" and
"cheap freelancer" -- the middle ground where quality and speed coexist.

- Client retention rate (customize: "average client stays X years")
- Revenue impact numbers (customize: "generated $XM in pipeline for
  clients in [year]")
- Team experience (customize: "combined X years of experience across

- Case studies with specific metrics (before/after, with client permission)
- Named client logos (with permission)
- Speed metrics (customize: "average time from brief to first deliverable:
