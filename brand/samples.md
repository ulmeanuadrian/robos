# Writing Samples — RoboMarketing

## 1. Social media post (LinkedIn, short-form)

> Echipa pierde 4 ore pe zi pe taskuri repetitive?
>
> Stiu, e un cliseu. Dar uite cifra reala dintr-un proiect din luna trecuta:
>
> — 27.000 lei/an pe angajat care face manual ce poate face un flux automat
> — 30 de minute pentru integrarea Stripe + SmartBill (versus o zi intreaga in Excel)
> — 0 erori de transcriere de cand a intrat in productie
>
> Automatizarea nu e despre "AI peste tot". E despre cei 4 pasi pe care nu mai trebuie sa-i faca cineva, niciodata.

**Demonstrates:** Tone (direct, anti-hype), Personality (numerate, anti-fluff), Rhythm (short opener, em-dash bullets, declarative close), Vocabulary (concrete tools: Stripe, SmartBill).

## 2. Blog intro paragraph (mid-form)

> Acum un an, primul meu reflex pentru orice automatizare era n8n. Astazi reflexul s-a schimbat.
>
> Nu pentru ca n8n s-a stricat — ruleaza in continuare in zeci de proiecte la noi. Ci pentru ca am dat de cazuri unde un workflow builder nu mai era cea mai buna unealta pentru job. Am tras concluziile pe propria piele, in proiecte cu clienti reali, si vreau sa le impart aici — nu ca verdict, ci ca observatie de teren.
>
> Iata cele 4 momente in care am ales sa scriu cod in loc sa trag noduri.

**Demonstrates:** Tone (founder voice, "eu"-driven, on-the-ground), Personality (operator-not-vendor, opinionated-but-evidence-based), Rhythm (short opener that sets up tension, mid-length explanatory body, em-dash asides), Vocabulary (n8n named, "nu ca verdict, ci ca observatie de teren" anti-hype phrasing).

## 3. Customer email (interpersonal, follow-up after WhatsApp call)

> Salut Mihai,
>
> Multumesc pentru discutia de azi. Recapitulez ce am stabilit ca sa avem ambii aceleasi note:
>
> **Ce automatizam in faza 1:**
> — Notificari WhatsApp la fiecare schimbare de status comanda (GoMag → WhatsApp Business API)
> — Generare automata factura SmartBill cand comanda trece pe "platit"
> — Tichete curierat Cargus, generate la "expediat"
>
> **Timeline:** 7 zile lucratoare de la confirmarea acceptului.
> **Cost lunar dupa lansare:** 22$/luna fix, indiferent de volum.
>
> Daca esti de acord cu scope-ul, raspunde-mi la mesajul asta cu "ok" si pornesc maine. Daca vrei sa mai ajustam ceva, pune un punct si discutam pe WhatsApp.
>
> Adrian

**Demonstrates:** Tone (partner-energy, no padding), Vocabulary (specific tools and stack named), Formatting (bullets for parallel items, bold inline labels, no emoji), CTA (informal but action-clear), Confidence Zones (committed pricing, committed timeline — no hedging).

## 5. Micro-copy — error message + empty state

> **Error message (form submit fail):**
> Nu am putut trimite mesajul. Probabil un timeout. Da-mi un minut si reincearca, sau scrie-mi direct pe WhatsApp: 0775 341 146.

> **Empty state (cron jobs page, no jobs yet):**
> Inca nu ai nicio automatizare programata.
>
> Cele mai utile pentru inceput:
> — un raport zilnic la 9:00 (research, monitoring, sumar inbox)
> — o automatizare saptamanala (postari, newsletter, raport vanzari)
>
> Adauga prima ta automatizare cu Cron New, sau cere-mi una pe WhatsApp.

**Demonstrates:** Tone (calm-confident, treats user as capable), Vocabulary (no apology theatre — "probabil un timeout" not "we sincerely apologize"), Rhythm (short, fragments where natural), Formatting (em-dash bullets, no emoji), Personality (operator-helpful, gives a concrete escape hatch).

## Cross-sample patterns to keep

1. **Number per paragraph.** Almost every sample carries at least one specific figure or named tool. If a draft doesn't, it's drifting toward generic.
2. **WhatsApp as the universal CTA.** It signals "talk to a human, not a form."
3. **No apology theatre.** Errors get one sentence + a path forward.
4. **Em-dashes for asides.** Use them. Don't switch to commas because it feels safer.
5. **Closing on action.** Every sample ends with a next step the reader can take in <5 seconds.
